Installation Guide

  • 1. Boot Image Flash
    Connect your device to a PC in Fastboot Mode, then run:
    fastboot flash boot boot.img
  • 2. Enter Recovery
    Reboot directly into the recovery mode:
    fastboot reboot recovery
  • 3. Prepare Sideload
    On your device recovery, navigate to:
    Apply Update > Apply from ADB
  • 4. Flash ROM
    On your PC, execute the sideload command:
    adb sideload filerom.zip
